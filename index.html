The System of Wasiyyat

In 1905, Hadhrat Mirza Ghulam Ahmad, the Promised Messiah and Mahdi (peace be upon him), authored Al-Wasiyyat (The Will), a divinely inspired book that established a sacred system of spiritual and financial commitment within the Ahmadiyya Muslim Community. It was revealed to him: "Inni Anzaltu Ma'aka Al-Jannah" (I have caused Paradise to descend with thee). Through this revelation, God instituted a dynamic system of two interdependent components: Al-Wasiyyat and Khilafat. Al-Wasiyyat requires continued struggle, sacrifice, and righteous conduct, leading to the reward of Khilafat, and together, they lead to Al-Jannah.

The National Wasaya Department's Role

The National Wasaya Department of the Ahmadiyya Muslim Community U.S.A serves as the administrative body responsible for guiding members through the Wasiyyat process, from initial application to ongoing support. The department assists Moosian with forms, financial calculations, and questions about their obligations, while also working to raise awareness about the spiritual significance of Al-Wasiyyat across communities nationwide.
